Our Services

Eiddo Power offers a broad range of renewable energy services designed to meet the needs of various sectors, from residential to industrial. Explore our offerings:

1. Renewable Energy Trading

Our team facilitates the buying and selling of renewable energy credits, helping businesses offset their carbon emissions and support sustainable practices. By participating in energy trading, our clients contribute to a greener future while meeting their environmental targets.

2. Solar EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction)

Eiddo Power provides complete Solar EPC solutions, managing everything from design and procurement to installation and commissioning. We ensure seamless project execution and high-quality results, allowing clients to harness the full potential of solar energy.

3. Operation and Maintenance of Solar Projects

Our operation and maintenance services ensure that solar installations run at peak performance. Eiddo Power offers regular inspections, maintenance, and troubleshooting, maximizing system efficiency and lifespan.

4. Solar Power Plant Design and Installation

From conceptual design to final installation, Eiddo Power specializes in creating efficient and scalable solar power plants. Our team considers all factors, including site assessment and system optimization, to deliver solutions that meet energy production goals.

5. Renewable Energy Consulting

Eiddo Power provides expert consulting services to help businesses assess their renewable energy needs and develop tailored strategies. Our consulting services include feasibility studies, financial analysis, and system planning to support smart and sustainable energy decisions.

6. Energy Efficiency Solutions

Our energy efficiency services focus on reducing energy consumption and optimizing performance. By assessing current energy use and implementing advanced technologies, we help clients reduce costs and achieve sustainability goals.

7. Power Purchase Agreements (PPA) Consulting and Facilitation

We assist clients in setting up Power Purchase Agreements (PPAs), enabling them to procure clean energy directly from renewable sources. Our team handles all aspects of PPA negotiation and implementation, ensuring seamless transitions to renewable energy procurement.

8. Renewable Energy Infrastructure Development

Eiddo Power provides comprehensive infrastructure development services for renewable energy projects. Our services include project planning, permitting, construction management, and support for both on-grid and off-grid systems.

9. Sustainable Energy Solutions for Commercial and Residential Projects

We design and implement sustainable energy solutions tailored to the needs of commercial and residential properties, offering scalable options that optimize energy generation and reduce costs.
