Our Services

At Eiddo Power, we provide a comprehensive suite of renewable energy services, catering to a diverse range of clients from residential to industrial sectors. Explore how we can empower your transition to sustainable energy solutions.

1. Renewable Energy Trading

Facilitate the buying and selling of renewable energy credits, helping businesses offset carbon emissions and meet sustainability goals. By participating in energy trading, clients contribute to a greener future while achieving their environmental targets.

2. Solar EPC (Engineering, Procurement, and Construction)

Manage every aspect of solar projects, from design and procurement to installation and commissioning. Our Solar EPC solutions ensure high-quality results, enabling clients to fully harness solar energy.

3. Operation and Maintenance of Solar Projects

Provide ongoing operation and maintenance services to ensure optimal performance of solar installations. Regular inspections, troubleshooting, and maintenance maximize system efficiency and longevity.

4. Solar Power Plant Design and Installation

From site assessment to final commissioning, we specialize in designing and installing efficient solar power plants. Our scalable solutions ensure reliable energy production tailored to your needs.

5. Renewable Energy Consulting

Offer expert consulting services to assess renewable energy needs and craft customized strategies. Our services include feasibility studies, financial analysis, and comprehensive system planning.

6. Energy Efficiency Solutions

Focus on reducing energy consumption and enhancing performance. Advanced technologies and strategic planning help clients cut costs and achieve long-term sustainability.

7. Power Purchase Agreements (PPA) Consulting and Facilitation

Assist in setting up and negotiating PPAs, enabling clients to procure clean energy from renewable sources. We handle all aspects of PPA implementation for seamless transitions.

8. Renewable Energy Infrastructure Development

Provide end-to-end support for renewable energy infrastructure projects. From planning and permitting to construction management, we ensure on-time and efficient project delivery.

9. Sustainable Energy Solutions for Commercial and Residential Projects

Design and implement energy solutions tailored to commercial and residential properties. Our scalable options maximize energy generation and optimize cost efficiency.
