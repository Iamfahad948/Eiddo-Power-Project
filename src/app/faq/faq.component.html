Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Have questions about renewable energy and how Eiddo Power can help? Here are answers to some of the most common queries. For more details, feel free to contact our team.

What is renewable energy?

Renewable energy comes from natural sources that are replenished continuously, such as solar, wind, hydro, and geothermal power. These energy sources are sustainable and eco-friendly, offering a viable alternative to fossil fuels.

How do I get started with solar energy?

Getting started is easy! Contact us for a free consultation, and our experts will assess your energy needs, provide customized design solutions, and guide you through the installation process.

What are the benefits of renewable energy?

Renewable energy reduces greenhouse gas emissions, lowers electricity costs, enhances energy independence, and promotes a cleaner environment. It is a sustainable way to meet today’s energy demands without compromising future resources.

Does Eiddo Power handle maintenance?

Yes! We provide comprehensive operation and maintenance services for solar projects to ensure optimal system performance and longevity. Our team conducts regular inspections and troubleshooting as needed.

How long does a solar installation take?

The duration depends on the project size and complexity. On average, a residential solar installation takes 1-3 weeks, while larger commercial projects may take longer. We’ll provide a detailed timeline during the consultation.
